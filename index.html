JOURNEY

Ever since I was young, I've lost hours on things I started, from mastering a piano song to fishing for that one shark in Animal Crossing under my blankets. That same persistence drives how I design today.

I'm drawn to the challenge of turning complex problems into meaningful, impactful experiences. Grounded in research and constant refinement, I design solutions that balance user needs with business goals, thoughtfully executed with hopefully a quiet sense of delight and honesty.

Currently studying Interactive Arts & Technology at SFU. I also design for Photocrumb, turning shared moments into memorable keepsakes.
